Learning to Learn

Jan 26th 2015

Throughout these past few weeks at the iron yard I have struggled alot with this idea of learning how to learn. The challenge for me is the battle to learn the languages and vocabulary of programming. I have always done well in the classes that are "conceptual." The ones that dont have a right "right" answer. My daily struggle is that even though there are always a few ways to solve a problem in code there is still fine details that are right and wrong.

I have never enjoed this type of detailed problem solving but I am finding myself excited on the few occassions I can successfully my mind allows me to grasp these new concepts I never dreamed of being able to. Growing up before going to bed my dad would always ask me "Did you learn something new today?" I think its safe to say he would be thrilled at how much I am constantly learning every day.
